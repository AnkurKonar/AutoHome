AutoHome
by Satyam Kumar (Satyam@TG) & Prasanjeet Keshri
FREE - It's Open Source


AutoHome enables you to convert your regular home to a digital smart automated home. AutoHome is the most seamless and cheapest home automation which doesn't replace your wires it just adds itself up in the circuit as a console and communicates with your mobile devices to turn appliance on/off or to give you report of their working. It also enables you to stream music or download important documents irrespective of the distance between your PC and your smartphone. It can give access even if you are on the other side of the globe!
